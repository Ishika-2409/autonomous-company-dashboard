## Talking Points

### When asked about the project:
"I created an end-to-end autonomous fleet analytics dashboard that demonstrates real-world data analysis capabilities. Starting with mock fleet data, I built a SQL database, processed data with Python pandas, and created interactive Power BI visualizations. The dashboard showcases 80+ KPIs including real-time operational metrics, predictive maintenance forecasting, and RCA analysis."

### When asked about challenges:
"Key challenges included data quality standardization, choosing appropriate forecasting models, and optimizing Power BI performance with large datasets. I addressed these through comprehensive data preprocessing, time-series forecasting with sklearn, and strategic use of aggregation tables."

### When asked about business impact:
"The analytics identified that 20% of maintenance issues account for 80% of business impact (Pareto principle). By focusing preventive measures on these high-impact areas, the company can reduce unplanned downtime by an estimated 35% and maintenance costs by 25%."
